Section B
Source & Map Task
⏲ ~10 min
📜
Historical Source: Florida Statehood (1845)

Read the following excerpt from a historical account describing Florida's transition from territory to state.

"When Florida became the 27th state on March 3, 1845, many settlers celebrated. They believed statehood would bring new roads, more protection from conflicts, and a stronger voice in Congress. But not everyone shared this joy. Enslaved people, who made up nearly half the population in some counties, had no voice in this decision. For them, statehood meant that the system of slavery would continue under new laws."
7Use the source above to answer the following question.
What does this source tell us about who benefited from Florida's statehood and who did not? Use details from the text.
8Think about what you have learned in Units 7–8 along with this source.
Based on this source and what you learned in Units 7–8, why might different groups of people have had very different feelings about the same event?
